CQ7 broad filter
================

mqtt && !_ws.malformed && mqtt.msgtype == 8 && tcp.dstport == 1883 && (mqtt.topic contains "+" || mqtt.topic contains "#")

Final verification in script:
- destination is the local broker, i.e. loopback address ::1 or 127.0.0.1 on TCP port 1883;
- the SUBSCRIBE topic filter has at least two wildcard characters in total;
- if a SUBSCRIBE packet has multiple topic filters, the request frame is counted once.
